Wordle Solver

A wordle solving app. Enter the words you guess from Wordle, or one of its adversarial twins Evil Wordle or Absurdle. After entering the word here, click each letter to change its color to white, yellow, or green. Once the word and colors match the game you're playing, click 'Finalize' to have wordle-solver determine what remaining words could be the solution.

Additionally, we have different algorithms avaialble in the Suggestions box to help you choose a next guess. Each one uses a different strategy. Mix and match to fit your play style!
